ABOUT US

Venom Collective is a streetwear fashion company. We design clothing with thoughtful messages behind them. Each month, we release certain exclusive designs that are only available for a limited time. These designs are never restocked. Our goal as a company is to become a household name when thinking of high-end streetwear. Venom is designed for everyone who is aspiring and working towards their dreams in life and believe in creativity and innovation.
